Big Stone County is one of the 87 counties in the US state of Minnesota. The county seat and largest city is Ortonville. The county has an area of ​​1,367 km² (of which 80 km² are covered by water), a population of 5 820 inhabitants, and a population density of 5 inhab / km² (according to the 2000 national census). The county was founded in 1862.
